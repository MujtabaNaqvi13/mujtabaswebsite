About Me

Hi! My name is Mujtaba Naqvi and I'm a Year 8 student with a big interest in technology and creativity. I started getting curious about coding a couple of years ago, and since then, I've been teaching myself new programming languages and skills.

I enjoy building websites, experimenting with design, and even playing around with advanced languages like GoLang and C#. One of my proudest achievements is creating this website, because it shows how far I've come in a short time.

I've also learned how to use GitHub to share my projects, which makes me feel like part of the worldwide coding community.

At school, I enjoy subjects like Maths, Science, and English, because they help me think logically and creatively at the same time. For example, Maths sharpens my problem-solving, while English helps me communicate ideas clearly.

Outside of coding and schoolwork, I enjoy discovering fun facts about computers and the internet, and I love finding the humor in technology through computer jokes.

My goal is to keep improving every year, learn more advanced programming, and maybe even create something people around the world could use. Right now, I'm proud to say I'm a student with a developer's mindset: curious, hardworking, and ready to face challenges.

I know this is just the beginning of my journey, but I'm excited for the future!
